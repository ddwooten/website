About Me

I have a passion for solving complex problems with simple data driven solutions.

With more than 7 years of Python and C/C++ development experience I know a thing or two about going from a customer need to a served solution. That road begins and ends with good teams and good teams are built on great communication. Having worked with many different people across different backgrounds and projects I'm look forward to finding out how we can work together on our most pressing challenges.

Aside from a lifetime of team-building and leadership, I have developed deep expertise in software development and project management. From whiteboard to user manual I have seen projects through every stage of the development pipeline. I've worked in Agile teams and I've worked in quality control teams. My focus is data-adaptive algorithms which use static or dynamic data to inform business decissions; i.e. machine learning.

From the heart of AI

A graduate degree from UC Berkeley

As the data science revolution began in the San Francisco Bay area so to did my graduate work as I started my Ph.D. at UC Berkeley in 2013. My thesis, already centered on the use of 100 GB+ data sets, quickly became interwoven with the rapidly expanding data community at UC Berkeley. Having been an original member of the Berkeley Institute for Data Science I more heavily integrated data science and machine learning into my thesis qualifying and earning my designated emphasis in computational and data science engineering.

About Me

I have a passion for solving complex problems with simple data driven solutions.

With more than 7 years of Python and C/C++ development experience I know a thing or two about going from a customer need to a served solution. That road begins and ends with good teams and good teams are built on great communication. Having worked with many different people across different backgrounds and projects I look forward to finding out how we can work together on our most pressing challenges.

Aside from a lifetime of team-building and leadership, I have developed deep expertise in software development and project management. From whiteboard to user manual I have seen projects through every stage of the development pipeline. I've worked in Agile teams and I've worked in quality control teams. My focus is data-adaptive algorithms which use static or dynamic data to inform business decisions; i.e. machine learning.

A Fellowship in Machine Learning

Fellow of the Insight Data Science class of 2020

Having completed my graduate work in December of 2019 I knew I wanted to begin my career in Machine Learning with the right network, and with the right mentors and so I sought out the Insight Data Science Fellowship.

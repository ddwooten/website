Emotional Sight

A computer vision system for identifying emotions in human faces

In short...

Working with a team of 3 other exceptional graduate students at UC Berkeley we built a computer vision system which not only identifies one or many human faces in live webcam video but, in real time, assigns emotional content to the human face(s) in question.

Pushing Product Faster

Transfer learning is one technique by which the time to the MVP can be cut significantly. Transfer learning takes advantage of prior-built models which may be related to the MVP and rather than train a brand new model from scratch, makes small adjustments to an existing model such that it may accomplish a different task than what it was originally designed for. For example, VGG-16 is a facial recognition model with more than 100 layers. We froze a large number of these layers in place and made small adjustments to the top most layers enabling a model created from VGG-16, with very little training time, to recognize not individual faces but the emotions displayed on individual faces.
